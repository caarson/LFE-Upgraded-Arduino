**Wire Configuration:**

* red wire = 5v



* yellow wire = 12v



* blue wire = signal



* white wire = signal



* orange wire = signal



* black = ground





**Pin Layout:**



* pin 5 -> SSR



* VCC is supplied via PSU to power Arduino + other sensors.



* MAX6675 temperature reader to pins: A2 -> SCK, A1 -> OS, pin 4 -> CS.



* PIN 10 SIGNAL TO MOTOR



* PIN 9 to SPEAKER



* PIN 8 to green wire for ATX power on (PWR-ON?)
